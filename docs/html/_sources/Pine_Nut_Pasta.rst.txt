----------------------------------
Pine Nut Pasta
----------------------------------

:guilabel:`Power Recipe`

Ingredients
-----------

::


    1 tablespoon olive oil

    1 pound Jimmy Dean sausage

    3 medium garlic cloves, minced finely

    3 1/2 cups water

    1/2 cup oil-packed sun-dried tomatoes chopped

    1/2 teaspoon salt

    12 ounces (about 3 1/2 cups) penne pasta

    1 (6-ounce) bag baby spinach

    3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

    1/4 cup toasted pine nuts


Directions
----------

Fire up instant hot water boiler, throw pasta in a pot and top with small amount of olive oil and salt.  When the water boils, pour it into the pot and set on low heat.

Heat the oil in a 12-inch nonstick skillet over medium heat until hot and add in the sausage. Cook until lightly browned and no longer pink about 5 minutes.

Stir in the garlic and cook until fragrant, about 30 seconds.

Stir in the sun-dried tomatoes, spinach and pine nutes and cook until spinach is wilted.

Add in the pasta.

Season with salt, pepper, and parmesan to taste.  Serve immediately.

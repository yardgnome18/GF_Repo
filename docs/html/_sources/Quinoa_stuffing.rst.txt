---------------
Quinoa Stuffing
---------------


Ingredients
-----------

::

    1 C uncooked quinoa

    1 C chopped onions
    
    1 T olive oil
    
    3 garlic cloves minced
    
    1 red bell pepper
    
    1 C diced carrots
    
    3/4 C diced celery
    
    1 C diced zucchini
    
    1 1/2 C frozen corn
    
    1 1/2 t ground cumin
    
    1 1/2 t ground coriander
    
    1 T chili powder
    
    1/2 t salt, or more to taste
    
    1 C grated cheddar cheese


Preparation
-----------
1. Cook the qunioa.  In a skillet, warm the olive oil and saute the onions and garlic on medium heat for about 5 minutes, until the onions have softened. 

2. Stir in the cumin, coriander, chili powder, salt, red bell pepper, carrots, celery, zucchini, and corn.  Cover the pan and cook for about 10 minutes, until the vegetables are very tender.

3. Combine the quinoa and the vegetables.  Serve topped with cheddar cheese.

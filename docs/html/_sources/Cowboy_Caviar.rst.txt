-------------
Cowboy Caviar
-------------

Ingredients
------------

::


    1 can black eyed peas, rinsed and drained

    1 can yellow corn

    1/2 sweet onion, chopped

    2/3 C green onion, chopped

    2/3 C cilantro, chopped

    1/3 C olive oil

    1/4 C red wine vinegar

    1 t cumin

    2 medium avacadoes, chopped

    salt and pepper to taste"


Instructions
--------------
Combine! Eat!

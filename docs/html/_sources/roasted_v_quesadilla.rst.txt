------------------------
Roasted Veg Quesadilla
------------------------

Ingredients
-------------

::

    2 cups quartered and sliced potatoes
    
    1/2 cup sliced onions
    
    3/4 cup sliced red or green bell peppers
    
    1 1/2 cups sliced zucchini
    
    2 tablespoons olive oil
    
    1 garlic clove, minced or pressed
    
    1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
    
    1 1/2 teaspoon salt
    
    1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
    
    4 corn tortillas (8 inches across)
    
    1 cup grated Cheddar cheese

Oven
---------------
425 for 25 to 30 minutes

Instructions
---------------

Toss all of the vegetables with the olive oil, garlic, oregano, salt, and black pepper until evenly coated. 

Spread them on a large pyrex and roast for 25 to 30 minutes until beginning to brown.

Warm tortillas in a skillet, then add cheese and vegetables while on the heat and fry until cheese is melted.

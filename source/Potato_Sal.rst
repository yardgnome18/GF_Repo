-----------------------
Potato Salad (Awesome)
-----------------------

Ingredients
------------

::


    2 lbs red potatoes

    3/4 C mayo

    1/2 T dijon mustard

    1/3 C dill relish

    1 t pepper

    1 t salt

    2 T parsley

    2 T green onion

    3 celery stalks, chopped

    1/2 C black olives

Instructions
-------------

Cube and cook the potatoes, cool.

Whisk together mayo, mustard, relish, salt and pepper.

Toss potatoes with parsley, onion, celery, olives, and dressing.
